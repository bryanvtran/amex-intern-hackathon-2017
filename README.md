### *presenting... Just Coworkers*

Easy way to match interns/ new hires with potential teams!
With out highly sophisticated matching algorithm, new hires will be matched with the best team for them!

- Very nice login page!
- Personal profiles for new hires and teams!
- Guaranted matching!
- Messaging center to communicate with potential teams!
- Admin pages!
